Our Story

A Legacy Built on Flavor

Spice Garden was founded in 2005 by Chef Ramesh Thapa, who dreamed of bringing the authentic tastes of Nepal and the Indian subcontinent to the heart of Kathmandu. What began as a small family kitchen has grown into one of the city's most beloved dining destinations.

We source our spices directly from local farmers, grind them fresh every morning, and cook every dish to order. No shortcuts. Just honest, soulful cooking.
